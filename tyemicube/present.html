What is Tyemi Cube?

Tyemi Cube is a collection of tools made first and foremost for students.
It's style is based off of iPadOS 14 and, as the previous phrase suggests, it's designed mainly for ipad.

Pros and Cons

Pros: Has a bunch of useful tools, is regularly updated
Cons: can be a little weird at times, cannot be used offline.
